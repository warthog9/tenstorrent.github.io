For software setup, visit our [software setup](https://docs.tenstorrent.com/getting-started/README.html) page.

For BIOS updates, non-Tenstorrent drivers, and other hardware support, visit the SuperMicro [product page](https://www.supermicro.com/en/Aplus/system/4U/4124/AS-4124GS-TNR.cfm).

For support with the Tenstorrent add-in boards and related Tenstorrent software, you can visit the Tenstorrent [Discord](https://discord.gg/tvhGzHQwaj) server or contact [support@tenstorrent.com](mailto:support@tenstorrent.com) with additional questions.
